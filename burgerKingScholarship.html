About The Burger King Scholars program

The Burger King Scholars program is a scholarships that offers $1,000 - $60,000 to high school students and burger king employees and their families.

The Burger King Scholars program has a disclosed acceptnace rate but has offerd 34.6 million in scholarships to nearly 3,200 students in 2023 alone.

The Burger King Scholarship does not require an essay and awards the students with the boldest profile.

Scholarship Amount

$1,000 - $60,000

Scholarship Qualifications

min 2.0+ GPA, community involvement (sports, volunteering, clubs, etc.), plan to attend 2-4 year college in North America.

Scholarship Requirements

No essay, applicaion on the Burger King Scholars page
